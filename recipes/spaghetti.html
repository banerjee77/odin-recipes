One-Pan Dirty Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti, and that too one-pan dirty spaghetti is a savoury, 30 minute Southern comfort food that merges 'Cajun' style dirty rice flavours with Italian pasta. It featutes pork, CAjun seasonings and the 'holy trinity'(onion, bell paper and celery), all cooked in one pan with pasta directly in the broth, creating a rich, thick sauce.
